Let's know about some football countries

Argentina

Argentina football team is quite good in terms of skills, passing, footworks, team work and overall football.

Brazil

Brazil won the FIFA World Cup the most, 5 times in football history. They are extremely sporty nation when it comes to football. They hosted the last 2014 World Cup.

Spain

Spain football team is quite good in terms of skills, passing, footworks, team work and overall football. They invented a new passing football style called tiki taka.

Germany

Germany football team is quite good in terms of defense, counter attack and teamwork. They scored 7 goals against Brazil in 2014 world cup quarter final and secured that world cup against Argentina.

France

France football team is the latest Fifa World Cup Champion from 2018. They rose from little support to actually winning the world cup defeating many teams along the way.

Portugal

While Portugal may not be as good of a team, they have a very valuable asset the footbal world has to offer Christiano Ronaldo- The Superstar footballer and amazing performer.

Italy

Italy is the latest Euro Cup winner. They didn't get to participate in last world cup but their performance was really amazing in the Euro Cup.
